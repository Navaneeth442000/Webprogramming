College Of Engineering Vadakara(CEV)

The College of Engineering Vadakara (CEV) is an engineering college in Kozhikode district of Kerala, established in 1999. The first engineering college under the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE),Thiruvananthapuram, established by Govt. of Kerala, started functioning in June 1999. The college is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Courses Offered

  1. B.Tech
  2. Civil Engineering
  3. Mechanical
  4. MCA

Facilities

  • Digital Library
  • Central Computing Facility
  • Hostels
  • Innovation centre
